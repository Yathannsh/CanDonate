ABOUT US

CanDonate is a non-profit organization working towards saving the lives and futures of children suffering cancer by raising funds for their treatment and complete medication. The organization works in collaboration with Bhagwan Mahaveer Cancer Hospital & Research Centre – North India’s largest cancer hospital. Join us in our effort and make the future of India brighter by contributing your bit.

Our Patron

Bhagwan Mahaveer Cancer Hospital & Research Centre – North India’s largest cancer hospital.

Services

Help turn tears to smiles, together we create a special world.

Cancer Support

We help create world-class cancer services for young people in the india, providing life-changing care and support so young people don't have to face cancer alone.

Strength Together

We know that having cancer at a young age comes with its own particular set of challenges. Our services put the needs of young people first and allow them to face those challenges together.

Safety +

We provide a safe place where young cancer patients can be together to enjoy a fun time away from hospital, doing what young people normally do without a cancer diagnosis.

Awareness

We plan to use printed information alongside utilising the internet to spread our message of awareness. We will be looking to find corporate supporters & grants as well as organising & supporting fundraising events in aid to achieve these goals.
